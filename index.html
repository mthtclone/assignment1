Christmas Event

Join us on December 24 for a heartfelt Christmas celebration filled with cherished traditions, inspiring performances, and the true warmth of the holiday season. This special evening brings together our students, teachers, and families as they share music, creativity, and festive joy. We invite you to experience the magic of Christmas through beautifully prepared presentations that reflect the spirit of unity and togetherness in our school community.

DateDec 24, 2025

LocationOn Campus at ILC

International Leadership College

Gift Exchange

Participants will take part in a gift exchange where each person brings a wrapped present worth at least 10,000 Kyats. This activity encourages sharing, excitement, and surprise among everyone.

Style Showdown

We will have a mini fashion contest where students can showcase their best Christmas or winter-themed outfits. Awards will be given to the most fashionable participants, such as:

Best Dressed, Most Creative Outfit, Best Christmas Theme Look.

Marshmallow Treat Station

Students will be able to enjoy soft, delicious marshmallows. You may set it up as: Marshmallow feeding station, Hot chocolate with marshmallows.

Movie Night

A cozy movie night will be arranged with Christmas-themed films. Students can sit together, relax, and enjoy a festive movie after the main event activities.

Celebrate the spirit of Christmas at our college with a day full of fun, music, and holiday cheer! From exciting activities to delicious treats, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Scroll through the highlights of our festive event below.

Join our Secret Santa gift exchange and bring a wrapped present to share. Take part in the Style Showdown to showcase your festive outfits. Enjoy the Marshmallow and Hot Chocolate Station with delicious treats. Relax during the Festive Movie Screening and end the day with a memorable group photo.

Enjoy a variety of festive games and interactive challenges throughout the event. From ornament crafting to a holiday trivia mini-contest, everyone can join in the fun and make memories with friends.

Holiday Treats

Visit the snack tables for warm pastries, seasonal desserts, and fresh hot chocolate. Try our limited-time Christmas cookies and enjoy a cozy break between activities.

Enjoy the cozy Christmas!

SCHEDULE

9:00 AM

Opening & Welcome

Kick off the Christmas festivities with a warm welcome from the college principal and student council. Enjoy a brief overview of today’s exciting activities and surprises ahead.

10:00 AM

Gift Exchange

All participants join in a fun Secret Santa gift exchange. Each student brings a wrapped present worth at least 10,000 Kyats. Get ready for laughs, surprises, and festive cheer!

11:30 AM

Style Showdown

Show off your best Christmas or winter-themed outfit in a mini fashion contest. Awards will be given for Best Dressed, Most Creative Outfit, and Best Christmas Theme Look. Snap photos and cheer on your friends!

1:00 PM

Lunch & Social Time

Refuel with a festive lunch spread including seasonal treats, snacks, and beverages. This is also a great time to mingle, make new friends, and enjoy the holiday atmosphere.

2:00 PM

Marshmallow & Hot Chocolate Station

Indulge in soft marshmallows and creamy hot chocolate. Set up a DIY marshmallow feeding or make your own hot cocoa creations. Fun for all ages!

3:30 PM

Movie Night

Wind down the day with a cozy Christmas-themed movie screening. Sit back, relax, and enjoy a heartwarming story to end the celebration on a cheerful note.

5:00 PM

Closing & Group Photo

Gather everyone for a group photo to capture the memories of this joyful event. Farewell messages and announcements for upcoming college activities will follow.
